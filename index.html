About BiriyaniHUB

Hyderabadi Biryani, prepared with mouthwatering flavors from the Nawabi state is what we deliver at BiriyaniHUB. The authentic ‘dum’ cooking process, not only enhances the taste but also ensures the aroma to hit the senses.

“There is no sincere love than the love of food- George Bernard Shaw” is what drives us in the processing of a complete meal that is Biryani. Great pain is taken to maintain quality of the Hyderabadi Biryani and that can be felt in each bite of it. The care with which each handi is packed is enough to lock the freshness of the food in it. To spice it up, the earthen pots used for packaging makes it a delight for the customer.
